The Ceremony & Reception

The ceremony will take place at 10:30 AM at St. Stephen's Church.

January in Florida is about as nice as it gets for weather. Expect it to be like Early Autumn in New England.

After the ceremony, about 11:30 PM we will move to Enzo's on the Lake, an Italian Restaurant with a great garden and view of one of Florida's many lakes. Be prepared for fun and alligators! It's just a short drive from the Church to Enzo's.

The reception will conclude at 4:30 PM, but if you're up for it, there will be one last after party at Sourglass Brewing starting at 8 PM.
