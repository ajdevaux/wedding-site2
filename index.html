Noelle and A.J.

met in the most romantic of all possible circumstances: a science conference. Undeterred by his nerdy attempts at "flirtation", she agreed to give him a chance and go in a date with him. He proceeded to do his very best to show her all the best places in the city of Boston.

They could talk for hours while at dinner or on road trips across New England. They couldn't help admiring they way each other thought, and appreciating new perspectives and ideas about all facets life.

Their love of adventure would frequently separate them, as he left to hike the Appalachian Trail, and she would spend months at sea in the name of science. They always found each other again, sometimes on remote Pacific islands, or among the glaciers of the Alps.

On August 21, 2017 a solar eclipse crossed North America, and they travelled to Grand Teton National Park to view the event. There, atop Table Mountain, under the crescent sun, A.J. dropped to his knees and asked Noelle if she would spend the rest of her life with him.

Traveling to Orlando

A world-renowned resort destination, that literally has nothing to do with why the wedding is happening there!

Orlando is where Noelle calls home, and her childhood priest Father George has been kind enough to officiate the ceremony. When you leave the theme parks and resorts, Central Florida has beautiful natural attractions and old southern charm that is highly underrated.

Plus, the weather in January is usually fantastic.

If you're travelling from out-of-state, the best airport is Orlando International Airport.

Out of State Guests

If you're travelling from out-of-state, the best airport is Orlando International Airport. Ubers are expensive and public transportation not so great, so we recommend renting a car. There will be plentiful parking at the ceremony, reception, and after party venues. That said, we encourage carpooling and are happy to help facilitate!

Things to Do

Mid-January is an excellent time to visit the theme parks with good weather and smaller crowds! But if you are not into Mickey Mouse, here are some authentic Florida wonders:

  • Beaches: New Smyrna Beach (Noelle's favorite), Daytona Beach, and Cape Canaveral National Seashore (A.J.'s favorite)
  • Gators: Black Hammock Restaurant, Airboat rides (drive down state route 50 towards the beach - we like Captain John. It helps if you call ahead), Gatorland Park
  • Aquatic Mammals: Blue Springs State Park (Manatees, especially if it is cold out), Manatee Park in New Smyrna (better for dolphins this time of year)
  • Freshwater Springs: Swimmable year-round! Better yet, rent a kayak! Wekiva Springs, DeLeon Springs (save room for pancakes at the old mill), Rock Springs State Park
  • Oranges: In season in January! Visit Hollieanna storefront in Orlando, or drive out to Christmas, FL to see the groves
  • Shopping: Visit Park Ave in Winter Park, FL for browsing (be sure to try the chocolate popcorn at Killwin's candy store) Downtown DeLand, the Premium Outlets
  • Culture: The Enzian Movie Theater, the Maitland Art Center, the Polasek Museum, the Audobon Society, Leu Gardens, Kennedy Space Ceter
  • Further Away: St. Augustine, the oldest town in the US, via Mount Dora makes for a great road trip!

Lodging

The wedding will be held in Winter Springs, FL about one hour from Disney/Universal and from New Smyrna/ Daytona beach. The church is approximately a 30 minute drive from the Orlando International Airport.

If you'd like to stay near the festivities, we suggest the following:

  • Westin Lake Mary North , Lake Mary
    Close to the party and route I-4 for reaching the airport/parks/beaches.

  • La Quinta Inn and Suites , Lake Mary
    A bit closer to the reception and after party venues, near a nice shopping district and convenient to I-4.

  • Candlewood Suites , Lake Mary
    Next door to the La Quinta

  • The Alfond Inn , Winter Park
    A Boutique hotel near beautiful downtown Winter Park. A bit farther from the action but great if you want to relax and walk to restaurants, bars, and activities. Be sure to check out the Winter Park Boat Tour!

We also encourage you to check out AirBnBs which are very affordable in our area. Search for Oviedo, Lake Mary, Altamonte Springs, Winter Park, or downtown Orlando. If you want to stay near the parks search for Kissimmee. All of these locations are within a 30 minute drive from the festivities. Downtown Orlando in particular has become a foodie destination with a cool mid-century feel!

Central Florida is unfortunately lacking in good public transportation. We highly suggest renting a car from the airport if you will be flying in. If you would like to find a carpool buddy please let us know; we are happy to help.
